ABOUT RIELKIES

Rielkies is a new local brand that was established on 1 February 2021. Rielkies is a brand food products produced by native Muslim citizen that is 100% halal. The founder is a famous artist in Malaysia which is Aeril Zafrel. Rielkies come out with cookies and various flavor of chocotub.

Slogan:
"Lekat di Gigi, Lekat di Hati"

Vision:
To become the second food brand in Asean in 2026.

Mission:
1. Create a 30 new millionaires nationwide in 2021
2. Trains and produce about 10,000 entrepreneurs on Rielkies product in 2022
3. In 2023, we want to open new Rielkies branch at all state in Malaysia

Founder, Aeril Zafrel

Opening hours: Everyday from 8am to 7pm.
